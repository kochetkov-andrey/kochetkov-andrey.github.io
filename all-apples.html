Agridera's Exceptional Apple Varieties

×××××

At Agridera, we pride ourselves on developing premium apple varieties that combine exceptional flavor, crisp texture, and beautiful appearance. Our apples are bred to meet the highest standards of quality and taste.

Our apple breeding program focuses on three main categories: Dessert Apples, Cooking Apples, and Dual-Purpose Apples, each carefully developed to meet specific market needs.

Dessert Apples

Perfect for fresh consumption, our dessert apples feature a crisp texture and balanced sweetness. These varieties are particularly popular in European and North American markets.

Cooking Apples

Specifically bred for cooking and baking, these apples hold their shape well when cooked and provide the perfect balance of flavor for pies, sauces, and other culinary creations.

Dual-Purpose Apples

Versatile varieties that perform equally well for fresh eating and cooking. These apples are increasingly popular in markets where consumers value multipurpose fruits.
